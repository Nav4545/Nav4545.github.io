About

Me

I am an 18-year-old student from Vancouver, British Columbia, pursuing a Bachelor's
degree in Computer Science at the University of the Fraser Valley. My academic journey
is inextricably linked to a passion for technology and a lifelong fascination with
automobiles, which inspired the development of this game that captures my deep
interest in cars. This project is more than just a hobby; it demonstrates my passion for
innovation, problem solving, and coding. It enables me to apply computer science
principles in ways that align with my personal interests.As I progress through my
studies, I am motivated to discover how technology can enhance our experiences with
our passions, with the goal of seamlessly integrating the academic and automotive
worlds that fuel my creativity and ambition.
